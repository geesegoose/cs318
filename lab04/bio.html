Mooseman

It was mating season in '03.

Greg was searching for his mate,

Where, out of now where. Greg heard other males bellowing their call.

Greg met up with his potential mate, but there were 2 other male moose and one man.

The man was immitating a female moose, for he was hunting and seeking to draw a male to his location to kill.

The man and the other moose seemed to be in perfect harmony with their calls

The man and the 3 moose decided to form an A cappella band

But man and the other 2 moose developed a nasty habit of smoking pine needles and eating poison ivy

A fight broke out...

Now it's just Mooseman all by his lonesome
